Identity, Values and Strengths reflection.

Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

I worked as a Nightfill assistant at countdown and they would force a co-worker that was put on Light duties due to injuries to do heavy lifting and pulling of pallets on pallet jacks. In my mind this was just an abuse of power and taking advantage of someone that was new to the job and didn't know any better. The values I weighed up was fairness, Equality, caring, and Trustworthiness it wasn't fair that he was being convinced that it was his tasks and if it was anyone else that knew any better they wouldn't have been placed on those tasks. The person was New and I cared about his welfare and didn't want to see him injured and if I can't be trusted to speak up when I see someone being mistreated then I won't be able to be trusted with running a shift myself. I think this was the correct decision as it saved him from getting injured and taken advantage of by the shift manager on the shift as he was only doing it so more work would be done and making himself look better in the eyes of the store manager. I would always make that decision even though it was against my managers wishes and it was the correct thing to do so he didn't hurt himself severely as he was put on light duties due to Back issues.

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

Growing up in a very tight knit family always made me appreciate the concept of family and close friends highly. My father's side of the family is very militaristic going back many generations of both Law enforcement and military which has given me a strong basis of discipline and comradery when doing things like Sports, school groups and General friend groups. I have a strong sense of family first and loyalty due to recently becoming a father. I find that because of my upbringing I am very kind hearted and light hearted. I love to see the best in people and situations. This means sometimes I will try laugh about a setback than feeling engulfed by dread at it.
